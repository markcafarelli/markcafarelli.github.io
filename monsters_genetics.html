👾 Monster Genetics 🧬

Welcome to the Monster Genetics Simulator! These monsters have 9 different traits.

The first 6 traits follow standard Mendelian inheritance, meaning they exhibit a dominant/recessive relationship (just like the polled vs. having horns gene in cattle). The last 3 traits exhibit blending (just like the musculature trait in cattle).

Here is what you will be doing:

  1. Generate Parents: Randomly create the genetic makeup of two parent monsters and decode their phenotypes.
  2. Build Punnett Squares: Drag and drop the alleles to setup crosses for each trait.
  3. Roll for Offspring: Roll a 4-sided die to randomly determine exactly which traits each baby monster inherits!
